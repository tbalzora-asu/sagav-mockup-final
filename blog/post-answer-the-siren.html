Answer The Siren

October 2, 2025 • 3 min read

The Siren calls to you with briny grace: grapefruit, sea salt, and rosemary. A savory reveal that balances mystery with elegance.

Tasting Notes

  • Grapefruit
  • Sea salt foam
  • Rosemary
  • Sparkling water

Ritual

Build in a highball over ice. Rim lightly with fine sea salt. Garnish with a rosemary sprig.
