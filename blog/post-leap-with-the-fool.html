Leap With The Fool

October 3, 2025 • 3 min read

The Fool encourages you to begin—yuzu, lime, and green apple awaken curiosity. Draw, sip, and step into the unknown.

Tasting Notes

  • Yuzu
  • Lime
  • Green apple
  • Coconut water

Ritual

Shake with ice and strain into a coupe. Garnish with a thin apple fan.
